Why Cellular Energy Matters

Mitochondria are responsible for converting nutrients into ATP, the primary energy source used by cells throughout the body. When mitochondrial efficiency declines, people may experience fatigue, slower recovery and reduced vitality.

Certain nutrients such as CoQ10, resveratrol, acetyl-l-carnitine and PQQ are widely studied for their potential role in supporting mitochondrial function and energy metabolism.
